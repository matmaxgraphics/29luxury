ABOUT US

What makes a house a home? Love, trust, peace... Relatives who gathered around a table, friends who are excited about a movie or a match in front of TV or family members who run to school for some work in the early morning.

29luxury is a Europe based company with a branch office in Benin City, Nigeria and we specialize in the importation of home interior sets, doors, dinning sets, TV sets etc. Our manufacturers are the best in Europe.

As 29Luxury, we have been working to make every product they need available for their homes accessible and in addition we offer all kinds of products a house needs with the most economical prices and the easiest payment options.  We work with a 100% customer satisfaction focus, and above all, we give correct, honest and complete information as a basic principle in our customer relations. On this road we set off with the principle of absolute customer and employee satisfaction, we adopt the sensitivity of people while offering them the latest furniture trends and doors, state-of-the-art kitchen equipments and the most stylish home textile products, constantly innovating ourselves with a contemporary vision by closely following global interactions.  Contact us today for your home,office and hotel needs.
